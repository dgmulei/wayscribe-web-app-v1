    Moderate Fit: Community-Clinical

    Profile Alignment: Strong MA clinical hours fit this cohort, but research profile over-qualified.     Lower Fit: Patient-Centered

    Profile Alignment: Research emphasis mismatches this cohort's priorities. Competitive Benchmarks: - Stats competitive but profile misaligned - Research overshadows patient-centered narrative - Better positioned for investigation-focused programs Exemplar Schools: University of Chicago, UVA, Loyola Chicago (Consider 1-2 if mission resonates)

    Least Fit: Mission-Driven

    Profile Alignment: Profile doesn't match cohort priorities. Mission-Driven schools seek service-oriented candidates with deep community ties and health equity focus. This student's research emphasis and high stats suggest different trajectory. Why Not Recommended: - Research focus misaligns with service mission - Stats above typical Mission-Driven medians (could face yield protection) - Limited community health/advocacy narrative - Better options in Clinical-Investigative cohort Exemplar Schools: Morehouse, Howard, Meharry (Skip unless mission deeply authentic to student's story)
